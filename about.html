About Me

Hello! I’m Sankhadip Saha, a PhD researcher at UNSW Sydney. My work explores the fascinating realm of materials science, with a focus on energy storage, operando techniques, and the electrochemistry of advanced cathode materials.

I’m passionate about pushing the boundaries of sustainable technologies through a mix of experimental and computational research. Beyond the lab, I enjoy collaborating with researchers worldwide to help shape the future of clean energy.
